About Us
Who are we?
CIES Consultants PTY LTD T/A CIES Professionals, established in 2008, has grown and developed over the years to become a landmark firm in the immigration advice industry in Australia. Our extensive experience serves to minimize the hurdles and pitfalls involved in this process, as working with experts maximizes the chances of a successful outcome. The firm provides high quality, professional advice and service to clients wishing to access Australian visas and Educational services. From initial assessments to the processing and lodgement of applications, Our team of experts ensures the transition of migration is invariable.

We have been empowering students by arming them with the required knowledge to study in finest Universities/Tafe/Colleges & helping them to Migrate successfully to Australia since 2008. There has never been a more desirable option than CIES Consultants PTY LTD T/A CIES Professionals, if a client would like to migrate to Australia.
Why CIES?
CIES makes the whole process of studying abroad effortless. We believe that the services provided by CIES will prove to be practical and time efficient for students during the critical period of choosing the right university abroad. By promoting the best universities/Tafe & institutes in Australia and obtaining admission for you in one of them, we ensure that your future takes off to a flying start. All the information you need is available at our fingertips. Also, our working relationships with the best Australian universities and Visa processing agencies ensures that you are given top priority in these institutions which makes your application process easier.
